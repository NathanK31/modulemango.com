Puddle Privacy Policy

// Puddle — Water Tracker Pet · com.modulemango.puddle · last updated: june 23, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Puddle — Water Tracker Pet ("Puddle," "the App"), published by Module Mango ("we," "our," or "us"), handles your information. Puddle helps you build a hydration habit by tracking your water intake and tailoring a daily goal to you.

Puddle is local-first: by default, your data is stored only on your device and is not sent to us. Some optional features — Health Connect, weather-based guidance, ads, and Cloud Backup — involve additional data handling, each described below. This policy is specific to Puddle and supersedes our general Module Mango Privacy Policy for this App.

Information We Collect

Stored On Your Device

  • Hydration data: The drinks and amounts you log, your daily intake, and your progress toward your goal.
  • Wellness profile: Optional details used only to calculate your daily water goal — such as body weight, general activity level, and your typical waking hours. You may leave these blank and set your goal manually.
  • App settings & progress: Your preferences (units, language, reminders, beverages) and in-app rewards ("Drops") and pet progress.

This on-device data lives in Puddle's local storage. It is not transmitted to us unless you turn on Cloud Backup (see below). Reminders are scheduled by your device using local notifications.

Account & Cloud Backup (optional)

If you turn on Cloud Backup, you sign in with Google and a private backup of your Puddle data (such as your hydration logs, goal, and settings) is synced to our cloud (Google Firebase) so you can restore it on a new device. In that case we process your Google account identifier and email address to hold your backup. Cloud Backup is off unless you enable it, and you can sign out, turn it off, or permanently delete your account and its cloud data from within the App at any time.

Advertising

Puddle shows ads through Google AdMob (including optional rewarded ads you can watch to earn Drops). To serve ads, the advertising SDK may process your device's advertising identifier and limited device/usage signals. We do not use any health or fitness data for advertising. See Google's Privacy Policy; you can reset or limit your advertising ID in your device settings.

Location for Weather-Based Guidance (optional)

If you enable automatic conditions, Puddle uses your device location to fetch local weather from the third-party Open-Meteo service, so it can adjust hydration guidance for hot or dry days. Only the approximate coordinates needed for the forecast are sent to the weather service. Your location is not stored on our servers or used to identify you.

Health and Fitness Data

Hydration is health data, and Puddle can connect to Google Health Connect to share it with your other health apps and to personalize your goal. This integration is optional, is off until you turn it on, and requires your explicit permission in Health Connect.

Data Puddle Writes to Health Connect

  • Hydration (water intake): With your permission, the drinks you log in Puddle are written to Health Connect so your water shows up across the health apps you use.

Data Puddle Reads from Health Connect

With your permission, Puddle reads the following to adjust your hydration goal and provide wellness guidance (for example, suggesting more water on more active days):

  • Steps
  • Exercise & workouts
  • Active calories burned
  • Distance
  • Weight
  • Sleep
  • Heart rate
  • Resting heart rate

How We Handle Health Connect Data

  • Health and fitness data read from Health Connect is processed on your device to power these features.
  • We do not upload Health Connect data to our servers, and we do not use it for advertising.
  • We never sell health data, never use it for marketing, and never share it with third parties or data brokers for their own purposes.
  • Our access to and use of Health Connect adheres to the Google Play Health Connect Permissions policy, including its limited-use requirements.

Your control: You can turn the Health Connect connection on or off within Puddle, and grant or revoke any individual permission at any time from the Health Connect settings on your device. Revoking access stops further reading or writing; hydration entries Puddle previously wrote can be removed in Health Connect itself.

Not medical advice: Puddle offers general wellness guidance to help you build a hydration habit. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Consult a qualified health professional for medical concerns.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the App: Track your hydration, calculate and adjust your daily goal, send the reminders you set, and run pet/reward features.
  • Personalize guidance: Use optional activity, weather, and Health Connect signals to tailor hydration suggestions on your device.
  • Cloud Backup: Store and restore your data when you enable it.
  • Ads: Show ads (including rewarded ads) to support the App, without using health data.
  • Support & legal: Respond to your requests and comply with applicable law.

Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the service providers needed to run the optional features you enable:

  • Google Firebase — Cloud Backup storage and Google sign-in (only if you enable Cloud Backup).
  • Google AdMob — advertising.
  • Open-Meteo — weather forecasts (receives only approximate coordinates).
  • Google Health Connect — on-device, under your control; data is not sent to us.

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.

Data Retention & Deletion

  • On-device data: Kept until you delete it in the App or uninstall Puddle.
  • Cloud Backup: Kept while backup is on; deleting your account in the App permanently removes your cloud data and sign-in.
  • Health Connect: Managed by you in Health Connect; revoke access or delete written entries there at any time.

To request help with deletion, contact us at contact@modulemango.com, or see our account deletion guide.

Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit for Cloud Backup and weather requests. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure, but we work to safeguard your data.

Children's Privacy

Puddle is not directed to children under 13 (or the applicable age in your region) and we do not knowingly collect their personal information. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your data, and to withdraw consent. Because Puddle is local-first, most of your data is already in your hands on your device. To exercise any right, contact us at contact@modulemango.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will revise the "last updated" date above and, for material changes, provide a notice in the App. Continued use of Puddle after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
