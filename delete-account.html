Delete Your Account

// last updated: june 21, 2026

Request Account Deletion

You can ask us to delete your account and the personal data associated with it by sending us an email. To protect your account, we only act on deletion requests that come directly from the email address registered to the account.

Quick Guide

  1. Tell us which app. Let us know which Module Mango app the account belongs to (for example, Lenda).
  2. Email us from your account address. Send your request to contact@modulemango.com from the same email address you used to register. We can only delete accounts when the request is sent from the account holder's own (registered) email address — this is how we verify you own the account.
  3. We'll reply. We will respond to confirm your request, and we may ask for a few additional details to verify your identity before deleting the account.

Email us to delete →

What Gets Deleted

Once verified, we delete your account and the personal data we hold that is tied to it. Some information may be retained where required by law or for legitimate business purposes (for example, fraud prevention or legal obligations), as described in our Privacy Policy.

How Long It Takes

We aim to action verified deletion requests within 30 days. If we need more information to verify your request, the timeline starts once we receive it.
